Our Values

Silence: Embracing silence and building an interior life founded upon an honest desire to truly listen to others. Introspective and self-reliant.
Charity: Sacrificing to help others without seeking recognition. Humble, reverent, and generous.
Purity: Being pure in thought, word, and deed. Moral and a gentleman in the finest sense.
Honor: Living a life of personal integrity. Ethical, honest, and trustworthy.
Patriotism: Seeking knowledge about one's country and passing it on. Devoted to personal freedom, justice, and civic responsibility.
