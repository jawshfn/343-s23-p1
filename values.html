Our Values

Silence: He has the strength to embrace silence and the will to build an interior life founded upon an honest desire to truly listen to others. He is introspective and self-reliant.

Charity: A charitable man sacrifices of himself to help others and seeks no recognition in return. He is humble, reverent, and generous.

Purity: The man who is pure of thought, word and deed does not shrink from adversity or loft goals. He is moral and a gentleman in the finest sense of the word.

Honor: He lives a life of personal integrity thereby accruing honor. An honorable man lives up to his promises to himself and others. He is ethical, honest, and trustworthy.

Patriotism: He seeks to know his country and pass that knowledge to others. He is devoted to the principles of personal freedom, justice, and civic responsibility.
