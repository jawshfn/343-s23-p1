Why Join Greek Life?

Greek life helps develop one's academic, community service, and social initiative.

Greek life is based on the tenets of friendship, leadership, scholorship, and philanthropy.

Greek organizations offer opportunities to develop leadership potential and training.

Once initiated, you become a member for life!
