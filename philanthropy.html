Philanthropy

At JMU Alpha Sigma Phi runs a philanthropy event every year! Specifically, we raise money for RAINN!

RAINN is very important to everyone of us, the mission of RAINN is to carry out programs to prevent sexual violence, help victims, and ensure perpetrators are brought to justice.

Sexual assault is very real espeically around college campuses.

One event we do is pie-a-brother. Brothers volunteer on the quad where people then get to donate to RAINN to pie a brother in the face!

Did you know?

Every 68 seconds, an American is sexually assaulted. Every 9 minutes, that victim is a child.
